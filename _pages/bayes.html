--- title: "Bayesian methods" permalink: /bayes/ author_profile: true --- I am very interested in researching computational methods for Bayesian inference. For example approximate Bayesian computation (ABC), Markov chain Monte Carlo (MCMC) and sequential Monte Carlo (aka particle filters) for Bayesian inference.

These interests are reflected in my publications and my blog posts.

Bayes Nordics
To ease information on events related to Bayesian methods, data analysis and practice, I have created Bayes Nordics. This is a distribution-list that focusses on Bayesian events, activities and job-posts in the European Nordic countries (just because I happen to work in a Nordic country). Please join in! It is free!
